Who We Are
Mocan Espresso Limited is a Kenyan company specializing in the cultivation and export of the finest coffee beans from Kenya to the world. Located in Nairobi, we have an extensive branch network across the country, working directly with smallholder farmers from the coffee rich regions. Our extensive global networks provide a ready market for coffee farmers who meet our quality standards. Our professional and well-organized handling of shipping logistics ensures that coffee beans are promptly collected from coffee farms and timely deliveries made to our esteemed customers across the world.
