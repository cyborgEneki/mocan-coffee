How we do it
Before we export our coffee beans, we make sure that quality is maintained right from cultivation using sustainable and environmentally friendly methods, to packaging for export.
  • We cultivate the coffee on our own farms and in conjunction with other coffee farmers
  • We harvest the coffee cherries at maturity
  • We sort the cherries, wash them and dry to form parchment
  • We then grade and price the coffee
  • We source for buyers in different international markets
  • Once we find a buyer, we take care of the shipping logistics and export the coffee to the target customer
  • We then ensure that prompt payments to the coffee farmers are made
